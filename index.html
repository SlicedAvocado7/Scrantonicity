Scranton's only Police Cover Band.

Creating unforgettable memories.

Scrantonicity is the best cover band in Scranton, PA. They won several battle of the bands 6 years in a row. They are known throughtout the North East. The band was founded by Kevin Malone(drums & vocals). Later he was joined by Darryl(vocals & keyboard) and Andy(vocals & strings). When these three are not jamming, they help run Dunder Mifflin Paper Company.
